Investigation Feedback

  • We asked 13 students of SKCH CBSE how the investigation sessions can be improved
  • We are comparing results against 110 students from 6 schools

  • Areas for improvement in the session

  • Students finding audits useful or not

  • Problems identified and potential solutions
  • Whether students communicated with stakeholders who face issues
  • Any questions students felt were not answered during the audit
  • Students' confidence that solutions would be accepted
  • How students would create audits by themselves
  • Emotions felt at the end of the session
